Castle Attack [prototype]

2d, devtober, demo | 2017 | Released

Overview

My game submission for #devtober 2019. It’s a really short demo of the game idea I had. - Only two short level - At the end of 2020 I want to release it as a story game with puzzle elements. Idea:

You control two players at the same time, move them over the board to complete your quests. To do this move the player at the same time at the position of the quest object to attack this one with the players. Notice:

  • Your movement can be blocked
